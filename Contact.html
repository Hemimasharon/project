Contacting anime organizations

Anime conventions:

Visit the specific convention's website and use the contact form provided. For example, Anime STL has a contact form for general questions, programming, and marketing inquiries.

Anime stores:

Use the provided email address or contact form on the store's website. For example, Kuri Nyann uses the email address support@kurinyann.com and has a contact form.

Anime news sites:

Visit the website and look for a "Contact Us" or "Contact" page, often with a form to submit questions or concerns. Creating anime-related content

For general inquiries: If you have general questions about anime or manga, you can try contacting a professional writer through services like Fiverr.

If you want to contact an anime director:

You may need to contact them indirectly, possibly through a third party or by submitting inquiries through their agency or publisher. Some directors may not be directly reachable for unsolicited proposals or questions about their work.

If you have an anime idea:

You might be able to submit your idea to a company that produces anime. You will likely need to find a company that is accepting pitches and follow their specific submission guidelines.
